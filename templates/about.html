About

I'm a science journalist, and I live in Santa Cruz, CA.

At the moment I'm an intern at the Monterey Herald. I've also written for Science, Eos and Mongabay. My background is in geology--I hold a M.S. Geology from McGill University, and a B.S. Earth Science from the University of California Santa Cruz.

While modeling earthquake processes at the United States Geological Survey I picked up some computer programming skills, which I am interested in applying to journalism. These include web scraping, data visualization, and database management.

Starting in the new year, I'll be writing for Inside Science News Service. Keep an eye out for my work!
