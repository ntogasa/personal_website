About

I'm a science journalist and intern at the Monterey Herald.

My background is in rocks and earthquakes. As a geologist I've worked in some rough conditions, ranging from the scorching heat of the Mojave desert to the biting freeze of northern Quebec. And yet I love geology--after all, what could be better than unearthing a story millions of years old?

Nowadays you can still find me chasing stories about the natural world, although I now welcome and embrace topics from this century. You can check out my most recent work here.

Thanks for stopping by.
